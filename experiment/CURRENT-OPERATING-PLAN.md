# Crime Cartography current operating plan

Status: **current**
Updated: 2026-07-23

This is the forward operating plan. Older Batch 1, Earth One, same-day cohort,
and inherited experiment documents remain historical evidence only.

## Content lane

1. Launch the project page and channel introduction.
2. Produce and human-approve two dedicated-channel pilot remakes.
3. Hold the first live Q&A after the proposed evidence gate and publish Project
   Update #2 describing what feedback changed.
4. Expand the reliable loop to more cities, crime categories, comparisons,
   states, national/international datasets, and later formats.

Publishing does not wait for a project-subscriber threshold. No inherited cut
is approved for the dedicated channel.

## Collective lane

1. Project email requests and public GitHub Discussions open with the page.
2. Canonical milestone and expert threads collect public deliberation.
3. At 500 project requests plus sufficient operational evidence, review whether
   a capped email editorial beta is ready. A first active cohort may be 100;
   the email list may remain open.
4. Economic or governance rules remain inactive until versioned terms,
   professional review, identity/tax/accounting operations, an effective date,
   and participant consent exist.

Canonical discussion destinations are recorded in
`experiment/DISCUSSION-REGISTRY.json`.

## Operating body

- Now: local workstation, static website, and email.
- Later: one private VPS for the harness and read-only mailbox worker when local
  operation is no longer sufficient.
- No hosted subscriber database or public portal by default.
- Email remains the participant interface until a concrete requirement proves
  another surface is necessary.

## Release safety

- Every YouTube mutation must use a channel-scoped token that resolves to the
  separately owner-locked Crime Cartography destination.
- Legacy shared-token authorization is disabled.
- Every dedicated remake requires revision-bound human approval.
- Crime-data releases minimize victim-identifying detail and neighborhood
  stigma, preserve source caveats, and invite expert/community-impact review.

## Current external gates

- Dedicated Crime Cartography OAuth connection.
- EmailJS recipient/template verification and Gmail read-only OAuth.
- Human approval/recording of the introduction.
- First dedicated city remake and encoded-artifact review.
- Professional review before any operative economic stage.
