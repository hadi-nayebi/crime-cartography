# Expert review: legal, tax, labor, privacy, and governance

This room asks specialists to identify legal and operational questions that the
project must resolve before any editorial cohort or economic model becomes
operative. Public comments are project feedback, not retained professional
advice or a professional-client relationship.

## Current design

- a public website and GitHub Discussions;
- email requests stored in a private mailbox;
- possible pre-publication editorial tasks;
- contribution records and a future, currently inactive value model.

## Known risk areas

- worker, contractor, volunteer, contest, and securities classification;
- tax reporting, identity, eligibility, accounting, and disputes;
- consent, retention, unsubscribe, confidentiality, feedback rights, and
  redistribution of pre-publication material.

## Three questions

1. Which activities can be tested safely before formal contributor terms exist?
2. Which promises, data fields, or workflow steps should be removed or delayed?
3. What professional reviews and records are minimum gates before activation?
