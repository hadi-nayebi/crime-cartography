# Design the email editorial beta

The proposed editorial beta sends bounded pre-publication artifacts by email
and asks participants for specific human judgment. It is not active today.

## What is settled

- Project email subscription is separate from YouTube.
- Publishing does not wait for the cohort.
- Email is the first interface; no subscriber database or portal is created by
  default.
- Release remains human-controlled.

## What must exist first

- confirmed addresses, unsubscribe/suppression, and retention rules;
- revision-bound tasks, a published rubric, and an appeal path;
- privacy and redistribution terms for unpublished material.

## Three questions

1. What evidence beyond 500 project requests should prove that the beta is
   operationally ready?
2. What should the first active cohort cap be, and why?
3. Which first task best tests whether participant judgment improves a video
   rather than merely generating more text?
