# Launch the public project and first dedicated remakes

Crime Cartography will begin publishing without waiting for a project-subscriber
threshold. Hadi is the editor for the introduction and first remade pilots.

## What is settled

- `@CrimeCartography` is the only publication destination.
- The twenty inherited renders are reference cuts, not approved releases.
- The introduction and each new render require human approval.

## What exists

- Twenty sourced city pipelines and reference cuts.
- Zero dedicated remakes completed or approved.
- A remake contract centered on time, place, meaning, and visible limits.

## Three questions

1. What must the introduction explain for a new viewer to understand the
   experiment without hearing every future policy detail?
2. Should Atlanta and Boston be the first two pilots, or would another pair
   test the format more honestly?
3. What evidence should every first release show before it is called engaging,
   useful, and ready?

Useful responses name the exact claim, scene, city, or release criterion they
would change and explain why.
